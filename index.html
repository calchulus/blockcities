What's a Block City?

A Block City is a virtual world that you can fill with real-world buildings, or construct new ones by bonding two existing buildings!

The best part? Your city (and all of its buildings) are provably yours—forever! Everytime you sell, buy, or construct, your ownership rights are guaranteed on Ethereum’s unchangeable blockchain!

Own Rare Digital Buildings

For the first time in history, digital assets have provable scarcity—meaning, you can now buy virtual buildings with verifiable proof that only you own them.

No one can steal, forge, or impersonate your ownership. They’re provably yours until the end of time!
